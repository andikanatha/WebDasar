About Us

The condition of the pandemic (COVID-19) is increasingly affecting mental health, this is in line with what was expressed by dr. Hervita stated on the Indonesian Ministry of Health's website, "In the early months of the COVID-19 pandemic, 1 in 5 people in Indonesia aged 15 to 29 years thought of ending their life. Furthermore, 1 year after the pandemic by a different survey, data obtained 2 out of 5 people thought of committing suicide. Beginning in 2022 about 1 in 2 people will think about ending their life.‚Äù This concludes that many Indonesians experience mental problems or low mental health.

Mental health has an important meaning in one's life, with a healthy mental one can carry out activities as living beings. A healthy mental condition will help a person's development towards a better future (Adityawarman, 2010). Mental health is a state in which a person is able to realize his own abilities, can cope with the normal stresses of life, can work productively and is able to contribute to his environment (WHO, 2016).

Based on the description above, our team created a website for mental health (Me Doc), on this website users can consult, meditate, control themselves, and get information about mental health.
